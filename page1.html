Fundamental Road Safety Principles

Traffic rules in India aim to protect all road users. Key basics include:

  • Obey traffic signals (red = stop, green = go only if safe)
  • Maintain lane discipline
  • Use zebra crossings as a pedestrian
  • Give way to emergency vehicles
  • Follow speed limits in urban and school zones
