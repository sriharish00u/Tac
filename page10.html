📝 Your Commitment Matters – Make the Pledge Today

Road safety is a promise we make to ourselves and our loved ones. Every commitment to follow traffic rules is a step toward a safer tomorrow. Let's summarize what we've learned and make a pledge for safer roads.

🎯

Your Responsibility

Road safety depends on individual commitment. Every decision you make on the road affects lives – yours and others. Be responsible!

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Community Impact

Share what you've learned with family, friends, and your community. One person spreading awareness can save many lives.

🛡️

Defensive Driving

Always expect the unexpected. Stay alert, patient, and cautious. It's better to arrive late than never.

🌟 The Road Safety Pledge – Take It Now!

I solemnly pledge to:

  • 🚗 Always follow traffic rules and signals – without exception
  • 🪖 Wear helmet/seatbelt every time I ride or drive – no exceptions
  • 📱 Never use mobile phone while driving – safety first
  • 🍺 Never drink and drive – I'll always designate a sober driver or call a cab
  • ⚡ Never overspeed - speed limits save lives
  • 🦓 Always use zebra crossings and let pedestrians cross safely
  • 👶 Never leave children unattended near roads
  • 📢 Spread road safety awareness in my community

By taking this pledge, you become a champion for road safety!
