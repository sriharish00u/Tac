✅ Essential Safety Practices

Adopting these safety practices can significantly reduce the risk of accidents and save lives.

  1. 🪖 Wear ISI-marked helmet – Always wear a certified helmet on two-wheelers. It reduces head injury risk by 72%.
  2. 🎗️ Use seatbelts – All occupants should wear seatbelts in four-wheelers. Reduces fatality risk by 45%.
  3. 📱 Avoid mobile phones – Never use mobile while driving. Hands-free is also distracting.
  4. 🍺 Never drink and drive – Even small amounts of alcohol impair judgment. Take a cab instead.
  5. ⚡ Follow speed limits – 20-30 km/h in school zones, 50 km/h in urban areas.
  6. 🔄 Use indicators – Always check mirrors and use indicators before changing lanes.
  7. 👀 Maintain visibility – Use headlights in poor visibility, wear reflective clothing at night.
  8. 🛑 Stop at red lights – Never jump signals, even if the road seems empty.
⚠️ Remember: Safety equipment only works if used correctly. Helmet strap should be fastened, seatbelt should be snug across your lap and chest.
