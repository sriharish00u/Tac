Essential Safety Practices

  1. Wear ISI-marked helmet on two-wheelers at all times
  2. Use seatbelts in four-wheelers for all occupants
  3. Avoid mobile phone use while driving or riding
  4. Never drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  5. Adhere to speed limits – especially 20–30 km/h in school zones
  6. Use indicators and check mirrors before changing lanes
