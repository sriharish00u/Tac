⚠️ Common Risks and How to Prevent Them

Understanding these risks is the first step toward avoiding them. Prevention begins with awareness.

🚀

Over-speeding

Reduces reaction time and increases injury severity. At 80 km/h, a crash is like falling from a 7-story building.

📱

Distraction

Mobile phones increase crash risk by ~4 times. Even 3 seconds of distraction at 60 km/h covers 50 meters.

🍺

Drunk Driving

Alcohol impairs judgment, vision, and reaction time. Even one drink can be dangerous.

🪖

No Helmet/Seatbelt

Dramatically raises fatality risk. Unbelted passengers can become deadly projectiles in crashes.

🏃

Jaywalking

Pedestrians crossing unsafely cause many accidents. Always use designated crossings.

😴

Fatigue

Drowsy driving is as dangerous as drunk driving. Take breaks on long journeys.

🚨 Important: Prevention begins with personal discipline and awareness. Every road death is preventable.
