Common Risks and How to Prevent Them

  • Over-speeding – reduces reaction time and increases injury severity
  • Distraction – mobile phones increase crash risk ~4×
  • Drunk driving – severely impairs judgement; strictly illegal
  • No helmet/seatbelt – dramatically raises fatality risk
  • Jaywalking – pedestrians crossing unsafely cause many accidents

Prevention begins with personal discipline and awareness.
