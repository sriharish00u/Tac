📊 Quiz Answers & Explanations – Learn From Your Answers

Knowledge is power! Understanding why each answer is correct helps you remember these life-saving rules forever.

1. 🚦 Red Light

Answer: Stop completely behind the stop line
Red means STOP. Never cross when the light is red, even if the road appears empty.

2. 🪖 Helmet

Answer: All two-wheeler riders AND pillion riders
Both rider and pillion must wear helmets. It's mandatory by law and saves lives.

3. 🚶 Crossing Road

Answer: Stop, look left-right-left
Always stop at the edge, look both ways, and cross only when it is safe.

4. 🍺 Drink Driving

Answer: Dangerous and illegal
Alcohol impairs judgment. Even small amounts can be dangerous. It's a criminal offense.

5. 🎗️ Seatbelt

Answer: About 45-50%
Seatbelts reduce the risk of death in front-seat passengers by 45-50%.

6. 🦓 Zebra Crossing

Answer: Pedestrians to cross safely
Vehicles must stop for pedestrians at zebra crossings. It's the law!

7. 📱 Mobile Phone

Answer: About four times
Using phone while driving increases crash risk by approximately 4 times.

8. 🏫 School Zone

Answer: 20-30 km/h
Reduced speed protects children who may suddenly cross the road.

9. ⚡ Yellow Light

Answer: Stop if you can do so safely
Yellow means get ready to stop. Only cross if you cannot stop safely.

10. 🛣️ Changing Lanes

Answer: Use indicators and check mirrors
Always signal at least 30 meters before and check all mirrors and blind spots.

11. 🚑 Emergency Vehicle

Answer: Move to the left and stop to let it pass
Move to the left edge, stop, and let emergency vehicles pass. It's the law!

12. 🌙 Night Driving

Answer: Use low beam when approaching other vehicles
High beams blind oncoming drivers. Use low beams in traffic or when meeting vehicles.

13. 👶 Child Safety

Answer: Both A and B
Children are small (hard to see) and don't understand danger. Always supervise them!

14. 🏍️ Two-Wheeler Capacity

Answer: Only the number of people the vehicle is designed for
Overloading affects balance and control. Always follow the manufacturer's specifications.

15. 🔧 Vehicle Maintenance

Answer: Prevent accidents and breakdowns
Regular maintenance of brakes, tires, lights, and mirrors is essential for safety.

16. 🛑 Following Distance

Answer: At least 3 seconds
Keep a 3-second gap from the vehicle ahead. Double this in bad weather.

17. 🏥 Road Accident

Answer: Call emergency services and help if safe
First call 100/102/108, then provide basic help if it's safe to do so.

18. 🌧️ Heavy Rain

Answer: Reduce speed and increase following distance
Wet roads are slippery. Reduce speed by 30% and double your following distance.

19. 🚸 Flashing Yellow

Answer: Pedestrians have right of way, proceed with caution
Slow down and be extra careful. Pedestrians may be crossing.

20. 🛑 Police Signal

Answer: Stop immediately and follow instructions
Always obey police instructions. Failure to stop is a serious offense.

21. 🍺 Alcohol Limit

Answer: 0.03%
In India, the legal limit is 0.03% (30mg per 100ml of blood).

22. 🚴 Cyclist Safety

Answer: Use bike lanes when available and wear helmet
Cyclists should wear helmets and use designated lanes when available.

23. ⛽ Out of Fuel

Answer: Move to side and call for help
Move to the roadside safely, turn on hazard lights, and call for assistance.

24. 👓 Sleepy While Driving

Answer: Stop at a safe place and rest
Drowsy driving is dangerous. Stop, rest, or switch drivers if possible.

25. 📖 Long Journey Prep

Answer: Check vehicle, plan route, and rest well
Check brakes, tires, fuel, and plan your route. Get adequate rest before driving.

Keep Learning and Stay Safe!
