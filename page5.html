📌 Key Safety Messages to Remember

🪖

Wear Helmet

ISI-certified helmet saves lives. Always wear it properly strapped.

🎗️

Buckle Up

Seatbelts reduce death risk by 45%. Click it every time!

📱

No Mobile

Using mobile while driving increases crash risk by 4 times.

🍺

No Drinking

Alcohol and driving don't mix. Call a cab instead.

⚡

Slow Down

Speed kills. Follow speed limits always.

🦓

Use Crossings

Use zebra crossings. Stop for pedestrians.

🏢 Where to Display These Messages

Maximize impact by placing safety messages in high-visibility areas:

  • 🏫 Schools: Near entrances, playgrounds, corridors, bus stops
  • 🏢 Offices: Near elevators, parking areas, notice boards, canteens
  • 🏪 Markets: Near shops, bus stops, public areas
  • 🏥 Hospitals: Near entrance and parking areas
  • 🛣️ Highways: At toll plazas and rest areas
  • 🏘️ Residential: Common areas, club houses, gates
