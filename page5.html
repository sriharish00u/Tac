🖼️ Posters – General Safety Messages

Visual reminders are powerful tools for spreading road safety awareness. These posters convey important messages that can save lives. Share these messages with your family, friends, and community!

Essential Road Safety Rules

💡 Share Tips: Display these posters in schools, offices, community centers, and public places to spread awareness. You can download similar posters from the suggestions in sug.md file.

📌 Key Safety Messages

These are the core messages that every road user should know and follow:

🪖

Wear Helmet

ISI-certified helmet saves lives. Always wear it properly strapped - not just on your head!

🎗️

Buckle Up

Seatbelts reduce death risk by 45%. Click it every time - front and back seats!

📱

No Mobile

Using mobile while driving increases crash risk by 4 times. Keep your eyes on the road!

🍺

No Drinking

Alcohol and driving don't mix. Even one drink impairs your judgment. Stay sober!

⚡

Slow Down

Speed kills. Follow speed limits. It's better to arrive late than never arrive!

🦓

Use Crossings

Use zebra crossings. Stop for pedestrians. Every life matters!

🏢 Poster Placement Guide

Where to display road safety posters for maximum impact:

  • 🏫 Schools: Near entrances, playgrounds, and corridors
  • 🏢 Offices: Near elevators, parking areas, and notice boards
  • 🏪 Markets: Near shops, bus stops, and public areas
  • 🏥 Hospitals: Near entrance and parking areas
  • 🛣️ Highways: At toll plazas and rest areas
  • 🏘️ Residential Areas: Common areas, club houses
  • 🚌 Transport Hubs: Bus stands, railway stations
