📢 Extended Road Safety Slogans

Memorable slogans help reinforce important safety messages. Share these with your community!

  • 🦓 Use zebra crossing – pedestrian's right of way.
  • 🛣️ Safe roads begin with responsible behaviour.
  • ⚡ Alert today, alive tomorrow.
  • 📖 Follow rules, reach home safe.
  • 🚦 Respect signals – protect every life.
  • 📱 One second distraction – lifetime regret.
  • 🍺 Drive sober or stay alive – choose wisely.
  • 🐢 Slow is fast when safety is first.
  • 🪖 Wear helmet, save life – every time.
  • 🎗️ Click it or ticket – seatbelt saves.
  • 👀 Mirror, signal, blind spot – check all three.
  • 🏫 Kids at play – drive slow today.
💡 Share the Message: Slogans are powerful tools for awareness. Write these on walls, share on social media, or create posters!
