📢 Powerful Slogans – Words That Save Lives

🎨 Create Your Own Slogans

Great slogans are short, memorable, and emotionally impactful. Here are tips for creating effective road safety slogans:

✏️

Keep It Short

Short slogans are easier to remember. Aim for 5-8 words maximum.

💭

Use Rhymes

Rhyming slogans stick in memory. "Helmet on head, life ahead" works well.

😢

Emotional Appeal

Connect emotionally - mention family, children, or consequences.

👁️

Visual Imagery

Create mental images - "Speed thrills but kills" paints a clear picture.
