🖼️ Posters – Speed & Sensitive Zones

Special care is needed in sensitive areas like schools, hospitals, and construction zones. These posters emphasize the importance of reduced speeds in critical areas.

School & Hospital Zone – Max 30 km/h
No Mobile While Driving
10 Golden Rules of Road Safety
⚠️ Remember: Speed limits exist to protect everyone. In school zones, children may unexpectedly cross the road. Hospitals have patients who need peace and quick ambulance access.

🏫 School Zone Safety

School zones require special attention from all road users. Children are unpredictable and may suddenly run into the road.

🚸

Speed Limit

Maximum 20-30 km/h in school zones during school hours. Watch for children crossing unexpectedly.

🛑

Stop for School Bus

Stop when school bus stops. Never overtake a stopped school bus. Check for children getting on/off.

👮

Follow Marshals

Follow traffic marshal instructions during school hours. They help manage traffic for children's safety.

🅿️

No Parking

Don't park near school entrances. This blocks visibility and creates danger for children.

🏥 Hospital Zone Safety

Hospitals require quiet and quick ambulance access. Patients inside need peace for recovery.

🚑

Ambulance Access

Keep ambulance entrance clear at all times. Never block hospital gates or emergency routes.

🔇

Reduce Noise

Avoid honking near hospitals. Minimize noise to help patients recover peacefully.

⏸️

Parking

Use designated parking areas only. Emergency vehicles need quick access at all times.

🏗️ Construction Zone Safety

Construction zones have changed road conditions. Extra caution is required.

⚠️

Follow Diversions

Follow posted detour signs. Don't try to enter closed areas or use unofficial paths.

🦺

Workers Present

Construction workers may be on the road. Watch for them and follow flagman's instructions.

🛑

Reduced Speed

Speed limits are reduced in construction zones. Fines are higher for violations.

🚧

Uneven Surfaces

Roads may be uneven or have temporary surfaces. Drive slowly and carefully.
