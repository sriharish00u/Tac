📚 Fundamental Road Safety Principles

Traffic rules in India aim to protect all road users. Understanding and following these basics is essential for everyone's safety.

🚦

Traffic Signals

Red = Stop, Yellow = Get Ready, Green = Go only if safe

🛣️

Lane Discipline

Stay in your lane, use indicators before changing

🦓

Zebra Crossing

Pedestrians have right of way at zebra crossings

🚑

Emergency Vehicles

Always give way to ambulances, fire trucks, and police

🏫

School Zones

Speed limit is 20-30 km/h in school areas

🪧

Road Signs

Know and follow all warning, regulatory, and informational signs

💡 Tip: Always assume other drivers might make mistakes. Stay alert and practise defensive driving.
