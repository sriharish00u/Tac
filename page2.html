📚 Master the Basics – Your Life Depends On It

Knowing traffic rules isn't just about avoiding fines – it's about protecting lives. Master these fundamentals and become a responsible road user. Every rule exists because someone lost their life.

⚠️ Remember: Traffic rules in India exist to protect ALL road users. Following them is not optional – it's your moral duty.
🚦

Traffic Signals

Red: Stop completely behind the stop line.
Yellow: Get ready to move, do not cross if you can stop safely.
Green: Go only if the path is clear.

🛣️

Lane Discipline

Stay in your designated lane. Use indicators at least 30 meters before changing lanes. Overtake only from the right side. Never straddle lanes.

🦓

Zebra Crossing

Pedestrians have the right of way at zebra crossings. Vehicles must stop completely to allow pedestrians to cross. Never park on zebra crossings.

🚑

Emergency Vehicles

Always give way to ambulances, fire trucks, and police vehicles. Move to the left edge and stop. Do not follow or obstruct emergency vehicles.

🏫

School Zones

Speed limit is 20-30 km/h in school areas. Watch for children crossing unexpectedly. During school hours, follow traffic marshals' instructions.

🪧

Road Signs

Warning: Yellow triangles - indicate hazards ahead.
Regulatory: Red circles - must be followed.
Information: Blue rectangles - provide guidance.

🚗 Your Responsibility as a Road User

Every road user has a role to play in road safety. Whether you're a driver, rider, pedestrian, or cyclist – your choices matter.

🚗

Car Drivers

Follow lane discipline, use seatbelts, check mirrors, use indicators, maintain safe distance, never use mobile while driving.

🏍️

Two-Wheeler Riders

Always wear ISI-marked helmet, avoid racing, maintain balance, use indicators, never carry excess passengers.

🚶

Pedestrians

Use footpaths, cross at zebra crossings, look both ways before crossing, wear bright clothes at night, never walk on highways.

🚌

Public Transport

Board/deboard only at designated stops, stand in line, hold onto handrails, do not distract the driver.

💡 Tip: Always assume other drivers might make mistakes. Stay alert and practise defensive driving - expect the unexpected!

📋 Know the Rules – Know the Consequences

Traffic violations don't just result in fines – they cost lives. Understanding penalties creates awareness about the severe consequences of breaking rules.

🚨 The Hard Truth: Behind every penalty is a story of a life lost or a family destroyed. These fines are nothing compared to the price of losing a loved one.

🚫 Without Helmet

Penalty: ₹500 - ₹1,000
Consequence: 72% higher risk of fatal head injuries

🚫 Without Seatbelt

Penalty: ₹500 - ₹1,000
Consequence: 45% higher risk of death in crashes

🚫 Using Mobile

Penalty: ₹1,000 - ₹5,000
Consequence: 4 times higher crash risk

🚫 Drunk Driving

Penalty: ₹10,000 &/or 6 months jail
Consequence: Severe impairment of judgment

🚫 Over-speeding

Penalty: ₹1,000 - ₹5,000
Consequence: Reduced reaction time, severe injuries

🚫 Signal Jumping

Penalty: ₹1,000 - ₹5,000
Consequence: Major cause of intersection accidents
