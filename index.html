📚 Why Road Safety Matters

💔

Every Life Counts

Over 1,400 people die daily on roads worldwide. Each death is preventable through awareness and following rules.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Impact

Road accidents affect not just the victim but entire families. Children lose parents, parents lose children.

💰

Economic Cost

Road accidents cost India approximately 3% of its GDP annually in medical expenses and lost productivity.

🏥

Healthcare Burden

Thousands are seriously injured each year, requiring long-term medical care and rehabilitation.

📣 Core Slogans – Share These Life-Saving Messages

These slogans save lives when shared! Spread these messages in your community, school, workplace, and social media.

  • 🪖 Helmet on head – life stays ahead.
  • ⚡ Speed thrills but kills – slow down.
  • 📱 Don't text and drive – arrive alive.
  • 🎗️ No belt, no travel – click it or regret.
  • 🍺 Never drink and drive – choose life.
  • 🚶 Use zebra crossing – pedestrian's right.
  • 🏫 School zone – drive slow, stay alert.
  • 🔔 Give way to emergency vehicles.
  • 🦓 Stop, look, cross – every time.
  • 👀 Mirror, signal, blind spot – check all.
