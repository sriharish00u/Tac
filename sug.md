## 💡 Tips for Using Images

### Image Quality

- Use high-resolution images (minimum 800x600px)
- Prefer vector images for logos and icons
- Ensure images are clear and not pixelated

### Legal Considerations

- Ensure images are royalty-free or properly attributed
- Check license for commercial use
- Give credit where required

### Optimization

- Compress images for faster loading
- Use WebP format for web
- Maintain consistent image sizes for grid layouts

### Accessibility

- Always add descriptive alt text
- Ensure good contrast
- Don't rely solely on images for important information
